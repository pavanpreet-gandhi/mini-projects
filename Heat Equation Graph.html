Heat Equation Solution¶

Cosider the following heat equation problem: \begin{gather} u_{t} = \alpha u_{xx},\quad 0<x<1,\quad t>0\\ u_x(0,t)=0, \quad u_x(1,t)=0, \quad 0<x<1\\ u(x,0)=x,\quad t>0 \end{gather}

alpha = 1

A(n) = (1/2)*integral(x*cos(n*pi*x), x, 0, 1)
X(n, x) = cos(n*pi*x)
T(n, t) = e^(-alpha*t*(n*pi)^2)

u(x,t) = 0
for n in range(1,4+1):
    u(x,t) = u(x,t) + A(n)*X(n,x)*T(n,t)
u(x,t) = u(x,t) + 1/2
